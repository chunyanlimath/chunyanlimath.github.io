Research

Research Interests

  • Applications of machine learning, especially deep learning in various fields
    • Life sciences and Medicine: Digital Twin for Health
    • Chemistry: Chemistry-informed neural networks (CINN) and Quadratic neural networks (QNN)
    • Material sciences
  • Data-driven modeling and computation
    • Discovering PDE models and dynamical systems from data using deep neural networks
    • Neural ODE, Physics-informed neural networks (PINN), Deep Operator Network (DeepONet), Fourier Neural Operator Networks (FNO)
    • Recurrent neural networks (RNN), Long-short memory (LSTM)
  • Application of machine learning to scientific computing and computational science
    • Solving PDE models and dynamical systems with deep neural networks
    • Physics-informed learning, PINN, DeepONet, FNO
  • Numerical methods for partial differential equations and thermodynamics
    • High-order energy stable schemes, energy dissipation rate preserving schemes,asymptotic preserving schemes, symplectic RK method
    • Inveriant energy quadratization approach (IEQ), scalar auxiliary variable approach (SAV)
    • Non-equilibrium theories for thermodynamics, Generalizaed Onsager principle
    • Dynamic boundary conditions
    • phase field models
  • Dimensionality reduction method for machine learning
      Feature extraction and feature selection methods for high dimensional data
      Discover latent variables which are representations in lower dimensional space, PCA, VAE, DVAE
